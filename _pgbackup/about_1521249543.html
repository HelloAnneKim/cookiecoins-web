Today, non-governmental organizations rely heavily on fundraising for financial support. We seek to use decentralized blockchain technologies to help them raise more money and do it in a secure and transparent way.

That's why we created cookiecoin.

Using the example of a bake sale, we show decentralized blockchain technology can be applied to the this traditional fundraising activity. Instead of it being a one-time event, our implementation shows how cookiecoins, or IOUs for cookies, can be sold and traded ahead of a fundraising event. In this way, we can take advantage of public enthusiasm for charity and cookies to hopefully raise more money for a worthy cause.
